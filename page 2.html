About our University

The University of Sri Jayewardenepura (USJP), located in Nugegoda, Sri Lanka, is one of the country’s premier national universities.

Renowned for its excellence in education and research, USJP offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across disciplines including Management, Science, Humanities, Technology, Medical Sciences, and Engineering.

With a commitment to innovation and community service, the university plays a vital role in shaping future leaders and professionals in Sri Lanka and beyond.
